Brontosaurus, or just Brontie, was born in April 2019 in Bangor Michigan. She spent her first six months with her dino litter-mates and mom at my cousin's farm, until I brought her to Lexington in Nov. 2019. Brontie is a typical tortie and very sassy and independant. She loves going outside and hunting. Generally not a fan of other cats, Brontie has a new friend Liopleurodon, who she is slowly warming up to.
